## TCS NQT NUMERICAL ABILITY EXAM BLUEPRINT & TOPIC PRIORITY
*Prepared by: TCS Senior Examiner (10+ Years Experience)*
*Target Time-per-Question: 60 Seconds Benchmark*

TCS NQT utilizes a single integrated adaptive test pattern where your performance dictates whether you qualify for the Ninja, Digital, or Prime job profile. 

**THE PRIMARY CHALLENGE:** You cannot switch back to previous questions once you hit 'Next' (Question Locking). Time allocation and rapid topic-spotting are everything. The Foundation Numerical Ability section typically features 20 to 26 questions to be solved in 40 minutes.

### 🟥 TIER 1: HIGHEST PRIORITY (Maximum Weightage & High ROI)
These topics appear in the highest volumes (typically 2-3 questions each). Mastering these shortcuts allows you to comfortably crack them in under 45-60 seconds.

1. **Data Interpretation**
   - Subtopics: Tabular Data, Graphical Data, Pie Charts
   - Weightage: Very High (3-4 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Easy to Moderate (45 - 75 seconds)
   - **TCS Internal Insight / Trap:** TCS provides massive, intimidating calculation sets. Do not calculate exact values. Master percentage approximations and option elimination to stay under 60 seconds.

2. **Arithmetic: Percentages, Profit & Loss**
   - Subtopics: Successive percentage change, Marked Price & Discounts
   - Weightage: High (2-3 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Easy (30 - 50 seconds)
   - **The Shortcut:** Avoid using the traditional equation-based 'x' method. Use fraction conversion (e.g., 20% = 1/5, 16.66% = 1/6) to solve mentally.

3. **Work and Time**
   - Subtopics: Mandays (M1*D1*H1/W1 = M2*D2*H2/W2), Individual Efficiencies, Pipes & Cisterns
   - Weightage: High (2 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Easy (40 - 50 seconds)
   - **The Shortcut:** Never use classic fraction additions (1/x + 1/y). Use the Total Work LCM Method. It drops solve times down to 30 seconds.

4. **Ratios and Proportion**
   - Subtopics: Income/Expenditure ratios, Partnerships
   - Weightage: High (1-2 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Easy (30 - 45 seconds)
   - **The Shortcut:** Directly substitute the options into the ratio values. If a ratio demands a multiple of 7, check the options for multiples of 7 first before solving.

### 🟨 TIER 2: HIGH WEIGHTAGE BUT TIME-CONSUMING
These topics are guaranteed to show up, but they are conceptually heavier. With proper training, they can be tamed to fit our 60-second rule.

5. **Number System**
   - Subtopics: Divisibility Rules, Numbers and Decimal Fractions, HCF & LCM
   - Weightage: High (2-3 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Moderate (50 - 70 seconds)
   - **TCS Insight:** Rely heavily on unit digit analysis, last two digits, and divisibility constraints (especially rules for 7, 11, 13, and 19) instead of complete long division.

6. **Distance, Speed and Time**
   - Subtopics: Trains crossing, Relative Speed, Average Speed
   - Weightage: Moderate to High (1-2 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Moderate (60 - 80 seconds)
   - **TCS Insight:** Avoid unit conversion errors (km/hr to m/s). Use the proportional method: If speed is constant, Distance is proportional to Time.

7. **Arithmetic: Averages & Ages**
   - Subtopics: Weighted Averages, Age ratios over time
   - Weightage: Moderate (1-2 Questions)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Easy to Moderate (40 - 60 seconds)
   - **The Shortcut:** Use the deviation method from an assumed mean rather than calculating the absolute sum of large numbers.

8. **Allegations and Mixtures**
   - Subtopics: Milk-water replacements, Weighted balance
   - Weightage: Moderate (1 Question)
   - Solvability in 60 Sec: Moderate (50 - 60 seconds)
   - **The Strategy:** Master the cross-multiplication allegory diagram; it solves complex average and profit/loss questions in under 40 seconds.

### 🟦 TIER 3: MODERATE WEIGHTAGE & FORMULA HEAVY
9. **Elementary Statistics:** (Mean, Median, Mode, Standard Deviation). Shortcut: Mode = 3(Median) - 2(Mean).
10. **Simplifications & Calculations:** Strictly enforce BODMAS/VBODMAS.
11. **Mensuration & Geometry:** If Pi is involved, check if options are divisible by 11.
12. **Series and Progressions:** Memorize nth term and sum formulas to keep calculations programmatic.

### ⬛ TIER 4: CONCEPTUAL & ADVANCED
Low frequency in Foundation, but critical for Digital/Prime roles.
13. **Permutations & Combinations:** Focus on linear arrangements with constraints.
14. **Probability:** Focus on standard card decks and dice-rolling shortcuts.
15. **Clocks and Calendar:** Shortcut: Theta = | 30*H - (11/2)*M |
16. **Equations:** Use back-substitution of options instead of manual solving.
